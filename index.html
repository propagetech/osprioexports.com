ABOUT US

Our Story

“Osprio Exports Private Limited” is a renowned Food Stuffs Export company in India, established with the intent to serve a wide range of agricultural commodities all around the globe.

We are a leading Supplier, Trader, Exporter and a Certified member of Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) with a good Supply chain across the country and strong relationship with our international Buyers. Being among the leading food export companies, we guarantee that our services conform to international quality standards.
With a well-equipped infrastructure, well trained & qualified manpower and highly experienced logistic team for timely delivery of the consignments, we have excelled in providing quality food products to our customers. With the progress of time, we have widened the range of our products and we bring to you some of the finest qualities of Rice, Pulses, Spices, Sugar, Fresh Fruits, and Vegetables.

All products that are packed in our warehouse pass through stringent policies for quality control to ensure that food handling and trading adheres with international quality standards. We ensure a timely and safe delivery of the cargoes by Air and Ocean in the most fast, smooth and convenient manner.
